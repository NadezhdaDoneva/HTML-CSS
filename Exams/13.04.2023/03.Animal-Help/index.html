WE WORK TO:

  • End the cruelest practices toward all animals.
  • Care for animals in crisis.
  • Build a stronger animal protection movement.
  • Several affiliated animal sanctuaries providing direct care and medical help for animals in need.
  • Rescuers and caregivers with years of experience.
  • The world's greatest roster of experts in animal welfare.

How we work

Ending the cruelest practice

We respond to large-scale cruelty cases and disasters around the world, providing rescue, hands-on care, logistics and expertise when animals are caught in crises. Our care centers heal and provide lifelong sanctuary to abused, abandoned, exploited, vulnerable and neglected animals.
